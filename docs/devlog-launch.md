# SLASH TV: THE FINAL BROADCAST is live — the most-watched show on Earth needs a contestant

*For twelve years, SLASH TV was the most-watched entertainment program on the
planet. Tonight it's being cancelled — so the producers decided to go out
with a bang.*

**Play free in your browser, right now — no install, no signup, no ads.**

SLASH TV is a twin-stick arena shooter in the Smash TV tradition: you're the
last contestant on a zombie-infested game show, fighting room to room through
a TV network that has completely given up on health and safety, while an
announcer who feels nothing narrates your ratings.

## What's on tonight

**Three full episodes.** 24 hand-built studio rooms — makeup, catering, the
prop department, the pyrotechnics bay — each with its own enemy mix, branching
routes you choose door by door, and a boss at the end: a game-show host with
a shotgun-mic, a network executive who teleports between takes, and a hovering
AI camera-drone that predicts where you're going to run. Beat a boss and the
run rolls straight into the next episode, score and all.

**A new episode every night.** SYNDICATION mode procedurally builds a fresh,
fair studio from tonight's date — the same seed for everyone on Earth, with a
**global leaderboard**, so you're racing the world, not just your own best.

**Seven zombies and counting.** Shamblers, swarmers, sprinters, boomers that
chain-detonate, stalkers that fade out, spitters that shoot back, and rare
gold-ringed **elites** that always pay out a gift when they fall. Press C on
the title screen to meet TONIGHT'S CAST before they meet you.

**An arsenal the audience throws at you.** Pistol, triple-shot, SMG, shotgun,
minigun, piercing railgun, flamethrower, and a rocket launcher with a real
blast radius — delivered mid-fight by a studio audience that's rooting for
you. Probably.

**Bring a friend. Or a robot.** Press B for CAM-BOT, an AI camera-drone
partner who fights beside you and drags you back up when you go down — or
press H to host an online co-op game and send a friend the link. No install
on either end.

**A multiplier with no ceiling.** Every chained kill builds your streak, and
it climbs for as long as you go untouched — x10, x15, beyond. One hit halves
it. The greedier the streak, the worse the fall.

**Plays on everything.** Gamepad (recommended), keyboard + mouse,
keyboard-only, or touch — and on your phone you can Add to Home Screen for
true fullscreen, offline-capable play.

## The fine print

The whole game is hand-coded canvas and synthesized audio — no engine, no
asset packs, every zombie and sound built from scratch. It's free; if you
want to throw something in the tip jar, the audience appreciates it. All
complaints are automatically applauded.
